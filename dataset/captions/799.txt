Royal Mail PLC is the United Kingdom 's market leader in industrial transportation based on revenues . In its 2019 fiscal year , it generated 10.58 billion British pounds , which was far more than its nearest competitor - aviation service company Signature Aviation PLC . Established in 1516 , Royal Mail PLC operated as a public postal service provider for most of its history , before the government sold majority shares following the Postal Services Act 2011 .
