While rivals , Manchester City , may have run off with the Premier League trophy , it was city neighbors , Manchester United , who topped the average attendance table in the 2018/2019 season . An average of 74,879 watched every home match at Old Trafford , meaning that the Red Devils more or less sold out the stadium for every one of the Premier League home games . Best league in the world ? Premier League attendances have been on the rise for the last decade , with the 2017/18 season representing a peak in the figures .
