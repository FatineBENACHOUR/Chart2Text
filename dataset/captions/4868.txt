260 patients died on the organ transplant waiting list while waiting for a kidney transplant in the United Kingdom ( UK ) during the period 2018/19 , by far the highest number compared to other organs . In the year to March 2018 , 63 and 45 people also died waiting for lung and liver transplants respectively . Transplant situation in the UK The number of people waiting for a kidney transplant in the UK was over 4.7 thousand in 2019 , which was the highest for any organ by quite a distance .
