In 2019 , apparel sales accounted for the largest share of Under Armour 's worldwide revenue , with a 66 percent share . 21 percent of the company 's net revenue was made through footwear sales that year . Footwear sales setting the pace The strong growth of Under Armour 's footwear segment – rising seven percent since 2012 – has contributed towards the shrinking sales share of its apparel category .
