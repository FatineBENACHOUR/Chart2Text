In 2019 , the pharmaceuticals and health products industry in the United States spent the most on lobbying efforts , totaling to about 295.17 million U.S. dollars . In the same year , the automotive industry spent about 68.92 million U.S. dollars on lobbying . What is lobbying ? Put quite simply , lobbying is when a company or organization tries to influence government leaders to create legislation that will benefit the lobbyist in some way .
