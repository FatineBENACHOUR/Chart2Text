The statistic presents information on the fastest mobile games to reach 10 million downloads worldwide as of December 2016 . As of the measured period Super Mario Run was the leader , having reached ten million downloads in only one day after its release on December 15 , 2016 . Mobile games downloads – additional information In a global ranking of the top mobile games to reach 10 million downloads , Pokémon Go claimed the second spot in exactly a week after its launch by Nintendo .
