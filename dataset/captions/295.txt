Having signed a lucrative seven-year deal with Chevrolet before the 2014 - 15 season , Manchester United 's revenue from kit sponsorships reached 64 million British Pounds in the 2019/20 season . This deal is worth 19 million British Pounds more annually than the agreement between Etihad and cross-city rivals , Manchester City . Big money in kit sponsorships In the 2018/19 season , the value of the kit sponsorships of all Premier League clubs amounted to 349.1 million British Pounds .
