In 2018 the population of the United Kingdom was estimated to have been over 66.44 million with 55.97 million of these people living in England . Scotland had the next highest population , at 5.44 million people , followed by Wales at 3.14 million and finally Northern Ireland which had 1.88 million people living there in 2018 . Regions and Counties The United Kingdom is a patchwork of various regional units , within England the largest of these are regions which show how London , along with the rest of South East England had almost 18 million people living there in 2018 .
