Between April and June 2018 , the travel website OUI.sncf ( previously Voyages-sncf.com ) hosted the visit of about 12 million unique users per month . It was the leading travel website in France as of the second quarter of 2018 , just ahead of Boooking.com . Travel booking websites OUI.scnf is the online ticket selling platform for rail travel of the French company SNCF , which is also linked to other European rail travel operator as Thalys or Deutsche Bahn for instance .
