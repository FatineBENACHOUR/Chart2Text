Major League Baseball ( MLB ) is one of the biggest sporting leagues in the United States and its most valuable team , the New York Yankees , are also the most-followed MLB team on Facebook with almost 8.53 million followers as of February 2020 . MLB value and revenue In 2019 , the teams in Major League Baseball are valued at an average of 1.78 billion U.S. dollars . Total MLB league revenue in 2018 amounted to 9.9 billion U.S. dollars , the highest ever .
