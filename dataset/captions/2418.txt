The statistic shows the leading credit card issuers in the United States in 2018 , by purchase volume . American Express led the ranking in that year , with credit card purchase volume amounting to 754.92 billion U.S. dollars . Credit cards in the United States The concept of using a card for purchases has its roots in the early 19th century , but it was sometime after that in the 1940s that the first credit card was used in the United States .
