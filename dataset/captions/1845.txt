In the week ending January 9 , 2020 , the most streamed track on Spotify in the U.S. was ‘ The Box ' by Roddy Ricch with 18.95 million streams by U.S. users . Other songs to perform well that week were ‘ Circles ' by Post Malone and ‘ Yummy ' by Justin Bieber with over eight million streams each . Artists and streaming : a double-edged sword Whilst the ranking of most streamed songs changes each week , artists to frequently make the top five include chart-toppers Post Malone and Drake .
