Manchester City was the most represented team at the World Cup in 2018 , with 16 of its players participating . One of those players , Benjamin Mendy , came back with a World Cup winners ' medal as France took home the trophy after beating Croatia 4 - 2 in the final . Premier League players at the World CupThe Premier League sent the largest contingent of players to the 2018 World Cup , with 108 of the footballers at the tournament plying their trade in the English top division .
