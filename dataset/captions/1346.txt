In 2019 , the music album with the most unit sales in the United States was 'Lover ' by Taylor Swift , far outperforming all other records in the list with almost 1.1 million copies sold that year . Second was 'When We All Fall Asleep , Where Do We Go ? ' by Billie Eilish with 594.6 thousand sales , followed by 'Happiness Begins ' by the Jonas Brothers . Other artists with best-selling albums in 2018 The ranking also includes albums by artists who proved extremely popular in 2019 such as Ariana Grande , Khalid , and Post Malone .
