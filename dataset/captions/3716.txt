This statistic shows the buying behavior of shoppers in the United Kingdom when purchasing beauty products , according to a survey published March 2014 . Of respondents , 25 percent said they researched products online before buying in store , where as 3 percent of people use instagram , twitter or other forms of social media to give recommendations to the customer . Online sales have seen the highest market sales growth in the beauty and cosmetics industry , with the high street falling behind , with a growth of only 1.2 percent .
