There are a few countries around the world who have encouraged the penetration of wind energy into their electrical grids . For example , wind penetration in Denmark exceeded 40 percent , while wind generation in Ireland , Germany , and Portugal reached between 20 to 30 percent of their total electricity generation in 2018 . Wind energy penetration reached only about 6.5 percent in comparison , although the United States has installed the most wind power in the world , second only to China .
