While the Dallas Cowboys may have last tasted Super Bowl success back in 1996 , the Texas outfit is still by far the most valuable franchise in the National Football League . Latest estimates value the franchise at 5.5 billion U.S. dollars , some 1.4 billion U.S. dollars more than its nearest rival , the New England Patriots . Cowboys lead the way off the pitch ... In 2019 , the Cowboys ' franchise was valued at 5.5 billion U.S. dollars , whilst the average franchise value in the NFL stood at about 2.86 billion U.S. dollars .
