The total costs of Hurricane Katrina amounted to 161 billion U.S. dollars , which makes it the most expensive natural disaster to occur in the U.S. as of January 2019 . Hurricane Harvey and Hurricane Maria followed behind at 125 billion U.S. dollars and 90 billion U.S. dollars respectively . Hurricane Katrina Hurricane Katrina struck Louisiana on August 9 , 2005 and displaced hundreds of thousands of people from their homes in Louisiana , Mississippi and Alabama .
