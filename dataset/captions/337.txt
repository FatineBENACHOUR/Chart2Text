The postseason of the NFL is when things start to get really interesting – the knockout nature of the games with a place at the Super Bowl on the line means that the tension is ramped up . The New England Patriots seem to be unfazed by this added pressure as they have notched up an impressive 37 wins during the postseason , marking an NFL record . Playoff mainstays While the Patriots have notched up the most wins in NFL postseason history , it is the Dallas Cowboys who have made the most playoff appearances – their 33 appearances are just one more than the number of times the Green Bay Packers and the New York Giants have made it to the knockout stages .
