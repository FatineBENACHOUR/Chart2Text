Berkshire Hathaway was the most profitable property and casualty ( stock ) insurance company in the world in 2018 , with revenues amounting to 247.8 billion U.S. dollars . People 's Insurance Company of China and Munich Re followed behind with 75.38 billion U.S. dollars and 67.22 billion U.S. dollars , respectively .
