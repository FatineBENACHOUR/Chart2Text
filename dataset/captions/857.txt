The statistic shows the capacity of the stadiums of the German soccer Bundesliga in the 2019/20 season . The Signal Iduna Park of Borussia Dortmund has a capacity of 81,359 . Bundesliga stadium capacities - additional information The largest stadium in the Bundesliga is Signal Iduna Park , home to Borussia Dortmund , which has a capacity of 81,359 , making it the seventh biggest stadium in Europe .
