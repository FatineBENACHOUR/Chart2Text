In 2018 , J.P. Morgan Chase was the commercial bank with the highest revenue in the United States , with a total revenue of 131.4 billion U.S. dollars . Apart from J.P. Morgan Chase , Bank of America , Wells Fargo , and Citigroup also topped the ranking in that year . Commercial banking A commercial bank is a bank that offers financial services to private customers and companies such as accepting deposits , checking services or loans .
