This statistic shows the share of consumers agreeing with statements regarding their habits while shopping , according to a survey conducted with UK consumers in 2016 . Of respondents , over 81 percent stated that they look at products in stores but subsequently go online to find the best price , and nearly 52 percent tended to use the same retailers on a regular basis . The most popular day for browsing online and doing shopping is shown to be Sunday , where as the least popular day is Friday .
