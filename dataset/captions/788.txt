Leading the ranking of highest-paid celebrities between June 2018 and June 2019 is Taylor Swift , with an annual income of 185 million U.S. dollars . Following closely behind are Kylie Jenner and Kanye West , with 170 and 150 million dollars in earnings respectively . Taylor Swift and Kylie Jenner were the highest paid women in entertainment in 2019 , beating the likes of J.K. Rowling , Kim Kardashian West and Celine Dion .
