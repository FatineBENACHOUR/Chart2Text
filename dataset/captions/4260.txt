Which carbonated soft drinks flavor is the most popular in the United Kingdom ? Cola flavored drinks held by far the biggest share of the UK carbonated soft drinks market in 2017 . Cola flavored drinks were so popular among consumers that there was a 50 percent market share gap between cola flavored drinks and the second most popular choice , lemonade . Brands on the market Among fizzy drinks that were not cola , Fanta had the largest number of consumers with 9.2 million in 2018 .
